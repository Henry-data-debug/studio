---
description: Tracking rent collection, payments, and financial health
---

This workflow is for financial controllers and property managers to monitor income.

### Steps:

1. **Navigate to Accounts**: Click on **Accounts** in the sidebar.
2. **Review Stats**: View the high-level metrics at the top:
   - **Rent Collected**: Total successful payments.
   - **Rent Pending**: Payments expected but not yet received.
   - **Rent Overdue**: Critical items requiring immediate follow-up.
   - **Occupancy Rate**: Overall portfolio health percentage.
3. **Record a Payment**:
   - Click **Add Payment**.
   - Select the property, floor, and unit.
   - Enter the **Amount** and **Date**.
   - The system automatically links this to the active tenant in that unit.
4. **Monitor Tenant Status**: Scroll down to the **Tenant Financial Status** table to see the specific payment status for every active lease.
