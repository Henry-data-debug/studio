description: Managing properties, units, and portfolio health
---

This workflow explains how to manage your property portfolio and individual units.

### Steps:

1. **Navigate to Properties**: Click on **Properties** in the sidebar.
2. **View Portfolio**: You will see an overview of all properties with their total unit counts.
3. **Add a Property**:
   - Click **Add Property**.
   - Enter the property name, address, and type (Residential/Commercial).
4. **Manage Units**:
   - Click on a specific property card to see its units.
   - Use the **Unit CSV Uploader** on the main properties page to bulk-import units if needed.
5. **Monitor Occupancy**: The properties dashboard shows at a glance how many units are in each development, helping you identify vacancies quickly.
