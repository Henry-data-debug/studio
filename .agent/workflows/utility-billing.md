---
description: Recording water meter readings for utility billing
---

This workflow explains how to record utility consumption for accurate billing.

### Steps:

1. **Navigate to Utilities**: Click on **Add Water Reading** in the sidebar.
2. **Select Location**:
   - Choose the **Development**.
   - Select the **Floor** and **Unit**.
3. **Enter Readings**:
   - Enter the **Prior Reading** (from the last billing cycle).
   - Enter the **Current Reading** (from the physical meter).
4. **Save**: Click **Save Reading**. 
5. **Billing Integration**: The system recorded reading can then be used to calculate consumption and generate utility bills for the monthly statement.
