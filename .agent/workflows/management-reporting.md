---
description: Portfolio monitoring and transparency for management (bosses)
---

This workflow is designed for organization bosses to monitor the overall health and security of the operations.

### Key Monitoring Areas:

1. **Dashboard Overview**:
   - Navigate to the **Dashboard** for a real-time summary of total tenants, properties, and overall system status.
2. **Airbnb Asset Monitoring**:
   - Navigate to **Airbnb Monitoring**.
   - Review which units are designated for short-term rentals and their ownership status (Landlord vs. Management).
3. **Audit Trails (Activity Logs)**:
   - Administrators can navigate to **Activity Logs**.
   - Review a chronological history of all actions taken in the system, ensuring accountability and transparency across the organization.
4. **Portfolio Analytics**:
   - Use the **Accounts** page to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) like **Occupancy Rate** and **Rent Collection Efficiency**.
