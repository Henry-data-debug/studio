---
description: How to onboard a new tenant and set up their lease
---

This workflow guides you through the process of adding a new tenant to a property and setting up their lease details.

### Steps:

1. **Navigate to Tenants**: Click on the **Tenants** link in the sidebar.
2. **Add New Tenant**: Click the **Add Tenant** button in the top right corner.
3. **Enter Personal Details**: Fill in the tenant's name, email, and phone number.
4. **Link to Property**: 
   - Select the **Development** (Property) from the dropdown.
   - Choose the **Floor** and then the specific **Unit**.
5. **Set Lease Terms**:
   - Enter the **Rent Amount**.
   - Select the **Payment Status** (e.g., Paid, Pending).
   - Choose the **Lease Start Date**.
6. **Save**: Click **Save Tenant** to complete the onboarding process.

The tenant will now appear in the active tenants list, and the unit status will be updated automatically.
